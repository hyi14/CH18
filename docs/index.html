Life Expectancy Report

Overview

This is a brief report regarding life expectancy for each country from 1960 to 2015 (source). The data reveals that the country with the longest life expectancy was Hong Kong SAR, China, with a life expectancy of 84.3. That life expectancy was 33.4 years longer than the life expectancy in Central African Republic.
